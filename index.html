MERCY BEAT

We love music

Just a couple of teens living life to the fullest! Mercy beat is a band consisted of 4 members. Cameron Johnson the drummer, Elise Paterson guitar (chords), Elena Ketcheson who plays the Piano, and Maya Vink who also plays guitar, (Fingerpicking style) and bass. The band started in late 2021 and early 2022 for a school hosted event and ever since then they've started writing songs in Maya's in home studio. The songs written are a range of different genre's going from full on Pop-Punk all the way to hints of Musical Theatre. Special thanks to: Naomi Nason - Photographer, Evan Tzavaras - Photographer/Videographer, Frank Vink - Co-producer/Editor, Rita Sabiiti - Instrumental support/Music guidance
