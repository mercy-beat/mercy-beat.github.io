ELENA KETCHESON

MEET THE PIANIST/SINGER

Elena Ketcheson

Music, to me, has always been a huge part of my daily life. When I was little, you'd almost always find me with headphones in (connected to my iPod shuffle) and a book in my hand. I've also taken piano lessons for most of my life, but singing has always been more of a priority for me. I started choir for the first time in 1st Grade, and love it to this day. Recently I've been learning more about a capella music! Elise invited me to join a band with her for Personal Project and I absolutely loved it! Soon after, Maya talked about starting a band with Cameron, Elise and I. I now play piano/keyboard and also sing in the band. Recently, my bandmates have started teaching me to play guitar, so occasionally I do that as well. I have been in this band for about 8 months and it's become the highlight of my life! Writing my own music, learning guitar, trying to connect music theory from piano lessons to band rehearsals, and experimenting with different music genres have all helped me develop my musicality. I take a lot of inspiration from artists like Sara Bareilles, Alessia Cara, Alec Benjamin, Laura Pausini, Faouzia, Mindy Gledhillâ€¦etc. I love songs that have a lot of dynamic changes and contrasts in texture and volume to emphasize different parts of the song. My favorite artist is always changing, because the music I listen to changes a lot depending on what mood I'm in. Currently, I'm obsessed with Alec Benjamin's song Devil Doesn't Bargain! I also love musical theatre, like Wicked and Hadestown.
