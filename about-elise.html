MEET THE GUITARIST/SINGER

Elise Paterson

For as long as I can remember, I have loved singing. I would dream of being famous (now I know better haha) when I was younger but never actually took any action to learn more about music. When covid hit, I took that as an opportunity to start learning guitar, something I have also always wanted to do but never started. I remember the first chord I learnt being D major, and struggling a lot with playing the cheat version of B minor. Now those are the easiest chords for me to play! For my MYP Personal Project I decided to start a band with some friends and it was my first time really getting into music theory. After that, Maya let me know that she wanted to start a band with Cameron, Elena and I. I now play guitar and also sing in the band. I have been in this band for about 8 months and have loved it! I have developed so much of my guitar, singing and music skills, and even gotten into song writing which has been really fun. I take inspiration from artists like Lewis Capaldi, Calum Scott, Billie Eilish, Fritz Hager, James Bay, Clinton Kaneâ€¦etc. I love when songs have simple instrumentals but have really deep, meaningful lyrics. My favorite artist of all time is Ben Platt. His song Grow As We Go has stuck with me for years and I hope that one day I will be able to learn it on guitar! I also love musical theatre, like Dear Evan Hansen and Mamma Mia.
